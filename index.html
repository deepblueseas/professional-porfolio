About Me

Hello! My name is Tess McGovern and I'm very happy to be enrolled in the CWRU Coding Bootcamp. My educational background is in the humanities, as I have a bachelors from Connecticut College with a double major in history and philosophy.


In my time working in libraries after college, I often became the person coworkers and the community relied on for basic website maintenance and general technology troubleshooting. an a life-long interest in computers, video-games, and web-design, this seemed like an exciting career change. I am interested in seeing where a combination of coding and library sciences could go. Or maybe I'll embark on a completely different path!


In general, I love my cats, knitting, and movies - especially the horror genre.
