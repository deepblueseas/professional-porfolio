About Me

Hello! My name is Tess McGovern and I'm very happy to be enrolled in the CWRU Coding Bootcamp. My educational background is in the humanities, but I've always enjoyed spending time on and learning how computers work. So when it felt like time for a career change, coding seemed like the right choice. In the last couple of years, I worked in several local libraries, and am interested in seeing where knowledge of app designs combined with work in libraries could take me.

Or maybe I'll embark on a completely different path!

I love my cats, knitting, and movies - especially the horror genre.
